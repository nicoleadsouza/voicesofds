2022

AT UMASS AMHERST













MARCH 25TH, 2022

With Voices of Data Science, our mission is to provide a platform to amplify the voices of data scientists, especially those from underrepresented communities. We aspire to build a strong and inclusive network of data scientists with a shared vision to use data science for common good. This year, we celebrate and showcase the success of women (cis and trans) and non-binary data scientists. We welcome and value attendees of all identities.
